The case of having a website in today's world is very strong!

Lots of business owners assume that having a Facebook social media page for their brand or business is enough, but there are some problems related with the strategy, like you will always be dependent on Facebook social media, algorithms change, rules change and what's working for your brand or business today, may not work for your brand or business tomorrow. 


Having a website meest your customers expectations

- Now more than ever, your customers expect to be able to find information about your brand or business online. Most customers do research online before they make a purchase or use your services. If you don't have a website, you're sending a message that you're not interested in finding new clients or that you're company is stuck in the dark age and neither of these are messages that you probably want to be sending out to your customers.

You can control information and branding

- User reviews and comments are great, but you should have the final say about what information about your brand or business is presented to the public. Having a website for your brand or business instantly creates an official presence on the internet so that you don't have to depend on others speaking for your brand or business.

A website is more affordable than traditional advertising

- Many small brands or businesses rely on Yellow Page ads and listings to get their name out there. No one will tell them to remove their listings but purchasing an website will increase their brand or business and their brand or business will have a wider reach range to potential customers.

If you have a website, it belongs to you and you control the content and the message you want to be sent out to your customers!
